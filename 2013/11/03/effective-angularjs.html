4 Tools for Effective AngularJS Development

Several months ago, right after the latest Google IO conference in May 2013, I started playing around with Google’s AngularJS. Back then, I had this small fun-project (later called domainhack.me) in mind, which should soon become my very first AngularJS-based, productive project.

With AngularJS being on everyone’s lips nowadays, also dev-teams at Immobilienscout24.de started some first attempts, trying to adopt this new, hyped MVC framework for our company’s very own use cases. However, these tentative steps could not (yet) lead to a final decision for or against a productive usage of AngularJS in our stack…​

Howto: Effective AngularJS

Nevertheless, I would like to share four very useful tools, which can help speeding up not just the AngularJS project setup, but also automating the everyday AngularJS development livecycle:

  • Yeoman - Providing a collection of tools and best practices to make developing for the web even better, Yeoman (using the Yeoman AngularJS-Generator) helps setting up the scaffolding of a AngularJS project, including the auto-configuration of Grunt-tasks.

  • Bower - As a dependency manager, Bower allows you to forget about manual downloads and the management of external scripts and comes in as a Yeoman dependency.

  • Grunt - Being a JS-taskrunner, Grunt helps with building, previewing and testing projects. Like Bower, also Grunt works great with Yeoman!

  • Karma - Last but not least, Karma enables you to test your code against multiple real browsers. To setup a fully featured, testing-ready project there is nothing more to do than using the Yeoman AngularJS-Generator from above (which makes use of the Yeoman Karma Generator).

All in all, these four tools can give your upcoming projects a massive boost, speeding up your AngularJS development and making it probably as effective as possible. I will definitely try to use them on a regular basis. I am pretty sure that this stack of amazing tools is going to push the popularity of AngularJs even further!
